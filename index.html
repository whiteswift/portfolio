About

Darryl is a computer-based designer and creative coder working from the UK. He develops audiovisual systems, interactive installations, and motion graphics that sit between design and software.

With a background in computer science, his work explores new interfaces and production methods—prototyping tools, writing custom shaders, and building generative pipelines that translate sound, gesture and data into visual form.

Recent focus includes realtime graphics, VJ performances using TouchDesigner, and web-based artworks in Canvas-Sketch. Collaborations span culture and music, with an emphasis on nostalgia, surrealism, and expressive technical aesthetics.
